About the Band

Born from the gritty streets of Los Angeles, NEON REBELS delivers raw, unfiltered rock that hits you in the gut.

Since forming in 2019, we've been on a mission to resurrect the spirit of authentic rock music. Our sound blends heavy riffs, pounding drums, and lyrics that speak to the chaos and beauty of modern life.

Influenced by legends like Queens of the Stone Age, Royal Blood, and Death From Above 1979, we've carved out our own unique sound that's earned us a devoted following in the underground scene.

The Rebels: Jake "Voltage" Carter (Vocals/Guitar), Maya "Storm" Rodriguez (Bass), Alex "Thunder" Chen (Drums), and Sam "Neon" Williams (Lead Guitar)
